# Nova Philosophy

Nova is built for the human driver of agents. Everything it does is centered around the assumption that there is always a human in the loop. Consequently, it does its best to ensure the experience of the human driver is pristine.

## The Trifecta

Bash. Worktrees. Tmux.

I believe that with these three tools and some well-written skills, agents can pretty much achieve _anything_ and **everything**.
